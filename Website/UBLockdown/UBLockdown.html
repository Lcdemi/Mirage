About The Competition

Welcome to UB Lockdown v18! This cybersecurity competition brings together teams from across the nation to face off in a high-stakes environment where every decision counts. As part of the Blue Team, your mission is to defend against sophisticated cyber threats, ensuring the successful and secure launch of BullBook, a next-generation social media platform. The Red Team is lurking in the shadows, aiming to sabotage BullBook's launch with their arsenal of cyber-attacks.

Your team must protect critical services while working through various injects and challenges. The goal is not only to secure systems but also to troubleshoot, think critically, and respond to evolving threats in real-time. With points awarded for service uptime, inject completion, and CTF challenges, every moment is an opportunity to demonstrate your team's skill and resilience.

Will you be able to withstand the Red Team's relentless attacks and secure BullBook's future, or will the platform fall victim to digital sabotage? The clock is ticking, and the competition is fierce. It's time to prove your mettle in the world of cybersecurity!
