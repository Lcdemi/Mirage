Competition Scenario

The world of social media is on the brink of a revolution. BullBook, a next-generation social media platform, is preparing for its highly anticipated launch. Millions of users eagerly await its debut, but beneath the excitement, a storm is brewing.

Lurking in the shadows, the Red Team has set its sights on BullBook, determined to sabotage its launch and exploit its vulnerabilities for their own gain. With a sophisticated arsenal of cyber threats, they plan to infiltrate the platform's infrastructure, harvest sensitive user data, and manipulate content algorithms. Their ultimate goal: to undermine trust, create chaos, and prove that no system is truly secure.

With time running out, CEO Alex Sterling has turned to you, a team of elite cybersecurity professionals, to ensure BullBook launches safely and securely. Your mission is clear—identify and neutralize potential threats, fortify defenses, uncover any insider threats working from within, and keep the platform operational despite the relentless cyber onslaught.

The future of social media hangs in the balance. Can you stop the Red Team and ensure BullBook's success, or will the platform become the latest victim in the war for digital dominance?

Team Roles

🔵 Blue Team

You and your fellow team members. Your responsibility is to secure the computers and network to prevent attacks, while completing important tasks to keep the business running.

⚪ White Team

Consultants that will assist you with your operations. Available to provide advice and help you out to the best of their ability.

🔴 Red Team

Professional and hobbyist penetration testers conducting adversarial operations. Their goal is to assess and exploit the configuration of Blue Team networks.

⚫ Black Team

Deploys and manages the underlying competition infrastructure. Ensures the competition network is and stays functioning properly.
