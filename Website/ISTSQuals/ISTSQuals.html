Your Mission Awaits, Agent

Mission Briefing

Agents of the Historical Timeline Taskforce & Protection (HTTP), your expertise is needed now more than ever. Our intelligence division has detected dangerous alterations spreading across the timeline — history itself is being rewritten in real time. These disruptions have been traced back to the clandestine group known as the Hourglass Inversion.

As elite temporal security operatives, your mission is clear: respond immediately to incidents, reinforce mission-critical systems, and correct historical deviations as they occur. Every service you defend and every report you complete is essential to restoring and maintaining timeline stability.

This organization has spent decades preparing for this exact moment. Now it is your turn to step into the timestream and safeguard the past, present, and future. We hold the line so the line continues on.

  • Maintain service uptime for all HTTP monitoring systems
  • Complete assigned mission reports (injects) within specified timeframes

  • Submit Incident Response Report detailing field findings

Remember Agents: Every second counts. The timeline depends on your vigilance and expertise.
